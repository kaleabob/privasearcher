Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2024

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

PrivaSearch is built on a simple principle: your privacy comes first. We do not track, store, or sell your search data. This privacy policy explains how we operate.

TL;DR: We don't track you. We don't store searches. We don't sell data. That's it.

What We Don't Collect

  • Search Queries: Your search terms are never logged or stored
  • IP Addresses: We don't record your IP address
  • Cookies: We don't use tracking cookies
  • User Profiles: We don't create user accounts (optional)
  • Browsing History: We don't track where you go after searching
  • Location Data: We don't collect your location

What We Do Collect (Minimally)

Server Logs

Our server may log basic information for security and uptime monitoring:

  • Timestamp of requests
  • Response codes (HTTP status)
  • Error logs (for debugging)

These logs are automatically deleted after 7 days and never used for tracking or profiling.

Optional Settings

If you configure API keys (Serper, OpenRouter), we store them only in your browser's localStorage. We never see them.

Third-Party Services

PrivaSearch integrates with optional third-party search engines:

  • DuckDuckGo: No tracking, respects privacy
  • Bing: May use tracking (your choice)
  • Google (Serper): Requires API key, your choice

When you search, your query goes to the search engine you select. Their privacy policies apply.

AI Summaries

AI-powered summaries use:

  • Pollinations (Free): Respects privacy, no logs
  • OpenRouter (Optional): When you provide an API key

Local Storage

PrivaSearch uses browser localStorage to store:

  • Your UI preferences (dark mode, engine choice, etc.)
  • Optional API keys (never sent to our servers)
  • Chat history (only in your browser)

This is all stored locally on your device. We never access it.

Security

All connections use HTTPS encryption. Your data in transit is always encrypted.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Significant changes will be announced on our website.
